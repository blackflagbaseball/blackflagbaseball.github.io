About the League

Est. 2019 — Play with Honor, Win with Attitude

The Black Flag Baseball League is a private fantasy baseball league built for managers who take the game seriously. Ten franchises. Two divisions. One champion. We run a full-draft, head-to-head points format across a 14-week regular season leading into a four-team playoff bracket.

Weekly recaps, official standings, trade news, and power rankings — all right here on the official BFBL site. Have a trade dispute or rules question? Drop it in the league Discord.
