about the league

The Black Flag Baseball League is a 12-team fantasy baseball league built for managers who take the game seriously. Head-to-head points format across a full regular season leading into a four-team playoff bracket. Every week is a battle. Every point counts.

Weekly recaps, official standings, top performers, and league news — all right here. Have a trade dispute or rules question? Drop it in the league Discord.
